**Inventory & Stock Management System**

**SmartInventory** is a web-based inventory and stock management system designed for small and medium-sized businesses. The system provides a centralized platform to manage products, track stock levels, maintain supplier information, and monitor sales and purchase records efficiently. By automating inventory management, SmartInventory reduces manual errors and improves operational efficiency.

## âš™ Features

### Product Management
- Add, update, and delete products with essential details (name, category, SKU, cost, price, and quantity)  
- Search and filter products for easy navigation  
- Real-time stock tracking to maintain accurate inventory levels  

### Stock Monitoring
- Automatic adjustment of stock quantities following sales and purchases  
- Configurable low-stock thresholds with notifications  
- Clear indicators for stock status (In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock)  

### Supplier Management
- Maintain detailed supplier profiles  
- Link suppliers to products for purchase tracking  
- Track supplier-wise purchase history for reporting and analysis  

### Sales and Purchase Management
- Record and manage all sales and purchase transactions  
- Generate detailed transaction histories and reports  
- Monitor trends and insights for informed decision-making  

### Dashboard and Analytics
- Centralized dashboard displaying key business metrics  
- Visual analytics for sales performance, stock status, and best-selling products  
- Summary of low-stock items for proactive management
